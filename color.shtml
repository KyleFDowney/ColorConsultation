How my Online Paint Consultant Service Works

After you’ve chosen the Paint Color Package LINK TO TAB that fits your painting project, click on the Shop tab at top to purchase the package.

Upon processing of your payment, you will receive an email with an attached questionnaire. If you don’t receive the email, please check your spam folder.

Color Elimination

Don't let an undertone

undermind your vision

Just as the same color paint may look completely different in your home from your friend’s home due to lighting, accessories, trim and flooring. Paint colors can even vary from room to room in your own home.

This is due to the paint having warmer or cooler undertones and how these undertones interact with the undertones of your furniture, accessories, flooring and room lighting.

Although you might have already eliminated a particular paint color as you didn’t feel it worked in your home, it could be possible the same color with different undertones could work.

As an example, a gray paint can have undertones of blue, green or purple. If one shade of gray doesn’t fit well in your room, it is quite possible another shade of gray with different undertones could look beautiful in your room.
